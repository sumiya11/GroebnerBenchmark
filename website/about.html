Gröbner basis benchmark

About Systems Contribute Logs GitHub ☰

This is a repository of systems of polynomial equations that can be useful when developing and benchmarking Gröbner bases algorithms. It is maintained by Alexander Demin (asdemin_2@edu.hse.ru).

One focus are systems in commutative rings over fields that come from applications in dynamical systems and control, robotics, cryptography, to name a few.

What are the goals of this repository?

  • To minimize accidental errors in development.

  • To preserve history and give credit.

  • To track the frontier.

Why use plaintext for storing data about systems?

  • It is easy to maintain.

  • We do not know if there exists a standardized format.

What are the current limitations?

  • It is hard to provide certificates for GB.

  • Storing large systems is tricky.

  • Curation.

We do not have a solution for these at the moment.
