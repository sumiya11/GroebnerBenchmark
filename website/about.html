This is a repository of systems of polynomial equations that can be useful when developing and benchmarking Gröbner bases algorithms. It is maintained by Alexander Demin (asdemin_2@edu.hse.ru).

One focus are systems in commutative polynomial rings over fields that come from applications in dynamical systems and control, robotics, cryptography, to name a few.

What are the goals of this repository?

  • To minimize errors in development and benchmarking.

  • To accumulate benchmark systems and knowledge about them.

  • To track the frontier.

Why use plaintext for storing data about systems?

  • It is easy to maintain.

  • We do not know if there exists a standardized format.

What are the current limitations?

  • It is not easy to provide certificates for GB.

  • It is not easy to store huge systems.

  • Curation.
